Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 How does the free version work?
The starter plan gives you access to our open-source code on GitHub. You can self-host the application and use your own AI API keys. Perfect for developers and those who want full control.
💡 What AI models are supported?
We support Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4 (OpenAI), and Gemini Pro (Google). Professional and Enterprise plans include AI costs, while Starter requires your own API keys.
🔒 Is my data secure?
Yes! All processing happens client-side in your browser. Your content and API keys never leave your device. For hosted plans, we use enterprise-grade security with SOC 2 compliance.
⏱️ When will the paid plans be available?
Professional and Enterprise plans are coming Q2 2024. Join our waitlist to be notified when they launch and get early access pricing.
🔄 Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Absolutely! You can change plans anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at your next billing cycle.
